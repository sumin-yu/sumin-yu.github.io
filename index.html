Sumin YU

Seoul National University, Electrical and Computer Engineering, ysmsoomin@snu.ac.kr

I am a Master’s student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University, supervised by Prof. Taesup Moon.

I received my B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University, where my undergraduate thesis focused on algorithmic fairness for Alzheimer’s disease prediction.

My research has focused on AI ethics (with an emphasis on algorithmic fairness) and AI safety (including safe image generation in generative models and safety evaluation of multimodal large language models). More recently, I have been moving toward the intersection of AI technology, policy, and governance, aiming to bridge technical research with broader societal and regulatory perspectives.

Research Interests

Ensuring safe and trustworthy AI, with a focus on algorithmic fairness, bias, harmful content in generative models, and privacy risks.

AI governance and accountability for responsible and trustworthy AI.
